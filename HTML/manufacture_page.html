Training for Manufacturers

JMAC offers a wide-range of training programs for manufacturers. These range from overall management techniques to shop floor techniques and are catered for with an executive level, middle management level, supervisor level, and operator level focus. Taking into account the challenges faced by each individual client, we design a customized training program based on actual needs.
Our well-versed consultants offer training programs filled with know-how on problem solving for business challenges, leveraging their consulting experience. Please contact us for further information.
